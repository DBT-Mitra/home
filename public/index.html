Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

  • Direct money transfer to bank accounts
  • Eliminates middlemen and corruption
  • Faster and transparent process
  • Covers scholarships, subsidies, pensions

Aadhaar Linking

  • Links Aadhaar with bank account
  • Enables DBT functionality
  • One-time setup process
  • Required for government benefits

DBT Status Checker

Check DBT readiness before you begin the process.

Readiness Checklist

Before DBT registration

  • Aadhaar card details should be correct and current.
  • Bank account must be active and linked to Aadhaar.
  • Registered mobile number should be accessible for OTP.
  • Documents should match the beneficiary profile.
